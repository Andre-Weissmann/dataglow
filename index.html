Multivariate Outliers

Approximate on-device methods. Mahalanobis distance (Mahalanobis 1936) and Isolation Forest (Liu, Ting & Zhou 2008) flag rows that are unusual across several numeric columns at once. These are heuristics, not ground truth.

SPC Control Charts & Process Capability

Shewhart 3σ control limits (Shewhart 1931) and the Cpk capability index (Six Sigma). Points outside the upper/lower control limits (UCL/LCL) signal a process out of statistical control.

Provided AS-IS, without warranty of any kind. DATAGLOW runs entirely in your browser and makes no guarantee of correctness, fitness for a particular purpose, or non-infringement. All statistical and machine-learning output is approximate and should be independently verified before use.

The statistical and ML techniques used here are implementations of publicly published academic methods. No novelty is claimed over this prior art:

  • MAD modified z-score — Iglewicz & Hoaglin (1993)
  • IQR / Tukey fences — Tukey (1977)
  • Benford's Law — Newcomb (1881), Benford (1938)
  • Shewhart control charts — Shewhart (1931)
  • Process capability Cpk — Six Sigma (public statistics)
  • Isolation Forest — Liu, Ting & Zhou (2008)
  • Mahalanobis distance — Mahalanobis (1936)
  • Levenshtein distance — Levenshtein (1965)
  • Jaro-Winkler similarity — Jaro (1989), Winkler (1990)
  • Differential privacy / Laplace mechanism — Dwork, McSherry, Nissim & Smith (2006)
  • Missingness framework (MCAR/MAR/MNAR) — Rubin (1976)
  • Pre-analysis checklist methodology — popularized by Atul Gawande
